◆ ALGORITHMIC_SCALING_IN_ANALYSIS_FILTERBANK

#define ALGORITHMIC_SCALING_IN_ANALYSIS_FILTERBANK   7

Algorithmic scaling in sbrForwardModulation()

The scaling in sbrForwardModulation() is caused by:

  • 1 R_SHIFT in sbrForwardModulation()
  • 5/6 R_SHIFT in dct3() if using 32/64 Bands
  • 1 omitted gain of 2.0 in qmfForwardModulation()

◆ ALGORITHMIC_SCALING_IN_SYNTHESIS_FILTERBANK

#define ALGORITHMIC_SCALING_IN_SYNTHESIS_FILTERBANK   1

Algorithmic scaling in cplxSynthesisQmfFiltering()

The scaling in cplxSynthesisQmfFiltering() is caused by:

  • 5/6 R_SHIFT in dct2() if using 32/64 Bands
  • 1 omitted gain of 2.0 in qmfInverseModulation()
  • -6 division by 64 in synthesis filterbank
  • x bits external influence
